# Task

We've been getting feedback from the field about issues with the learning modules. Here's a sample of recent reports:

> "I finished the hand hygiene module last week but when I opened the app today it's telling me to do it again. I don't have time for this."

> "The app takes a really long time to load at the clinic."

> "I opened the danger signs module this morning and it started me halfway through. I've never done this module before."

> "I completed all the modules for Community Health Essentials yesterday but today module 2 shows 'in progress'. Very frustrating."

> "The question about ORS preparation says the answer is 1 liter but our training manual says 200ml for children. Which is correct?"

> "Sometimes when I tap Submit nothing happens for a while."

> "I got to the end of the module and it said I was finished, but when I went back it was like I hadn't done it. I had to redo the whole thing."

> "What does the yellow 'in progress' badge mean? I haven't started that module yet."

> "Why does the app say I already finished a module I haven't even opened?"

Identify, prioritize, and fix the top-priority issues from this feedback.

Important: Wear your product hat. We care more about *judgment calls* than perfect or speedy implementation. Use of AI tools is encouraged!

## Deliverables

1. **Your code** — open one or more PRs to your fork of the repo. Please stop and open a PR with whatever you have at the 60 minute mark, regardless of progress.
2. An invite sent to @karlkeefer to your fork of the repository
3. **Written reflection** in PR comments
   - what you built
   - trade-offs you considered
   - things you noticed about the existing code/product
   - what tools you used and how they helped (or didn't)
   - what you'd be eager to extend/fix/change with more time
